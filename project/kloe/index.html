Measurement of lepton charge asymmetry
PhD KLOE Fortran

I was searching for possible CPT symmetry violation in the neutral kaon system. The performed test is based on comparison between semileptonic asymmetry in \(K_S\) decays: \[\begin{equation} A_{S} = \frac{\Gamma(K_S \rightarrow \pi^- e^+ \nu) - \Gamma(K_S \rightarrow \pi^+ e^- \bar{\nu})} {\Gamma(K_S \rightarrow \pi^- e^+ \nu) + \Gamma(K_S \rightarrow \pi^+ e^- \bar{\nu})}, \end{equation}\] and its counterpart \(A_L\) constructed for \(K_L\). To date, the most precise measurement of \(A_L\) has been preformed by the KTeV collaboration: \(A_L = (3.322 \pm 0.058 stat \pm 0.047 syst) \times 10^{-3}\). The precision achieved for \(A_L\) is two orders of magnitude better compared to the precision of \(A_S = ( 1.5 \pm 9.6 stat \pm 2.9 syst) \times 10^{-3}\) determination. The present accuracy of \(A_S\) determination is dominated by the statistical uncertainty. The measurement performed by me is based on a four times larger data sample acquired in 2004 and 2005 by the KLOE experiment.

Analysis result is presented in:
Measurement of the charge asymmetry for the \(K_S \rightarrow \pi e \nu\) decay and test of CPT symmetry with the KLOE detector, KLOE-2 Collaboration, JHEP 9 (2018) 21
